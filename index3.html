Introduction Lessons In The News Toolkit

• Reuters is building a “cybernetic newsroom” to take advantage of the best capabilities of both machines and humans.

• End-to-end content automation often might not be quite right and can feel stilted and robotic.

• Examples of useful functionality:

Surface useful factual sentences to help a reporter provide context, such as “X hasn’t happened since Y date”

Provide clusters of such facts to help provide depth

Suggest story concepts for a reporter to write, especially so that person can write a more informed analysis piece

Provide building blocks that allow reporters to drag and drop market details or milestones into a story.
